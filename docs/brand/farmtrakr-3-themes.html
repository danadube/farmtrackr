FarmTrakr Brand Themes

Three warm, inviting design directions that make agents excited to use the app every day. Each features soft, curved typography and welcoming color palettes.

Option 1

Natural Harvest

Earthy, organic, and grounded. Warm forest greens with golden harvest accents. Features rounded sans-serifs for friendly approachability.

Typography

Heading Font: Quicksand
Welcome back to FarmTrakr
Soft curves and friendly letterforms
Perfect for dashboard headers and section titles
Body Font: Nunito
Nunito provides excellent readability with rounded terminals that complement Quicksand perfectly. It feels warm and welcoming without sacrificing clarity.
Great for labels, descriptions, and all body text throughout the app.
